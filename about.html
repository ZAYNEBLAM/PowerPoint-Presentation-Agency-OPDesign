about us

At OnPoint Design, we help companies worldwide deliver their message by creating impactful presentation materials on PowerPoint, Prezi, Google Slides, and Keynote. We create custom-made presentations with unique visuals for any industry and any occasion: sales presentation, financial reports, event presentation, e-learning, pitch deck presentation... We also provide PowerPoint training, template creation, video editing, and research services.

Process

How we work


Gathering Content

We receive your request and discuss your needs and design guidelines via email or phone to determine the best fit for your project.

Defining Proposal & Timeline

We draw our proposal and timeline that works best with your schedule and assign your project to our design specialists.

Reviewing Sample Slides

We provide you with sample slides for initial viewing and discuss adjustments if necessary.

Quality Check

Once our design specialists are done, our quality assurance team carries on with a detailed review of each slide.

Final Presentation Deliverable

Et voilà! Your presentation is ready and it is now time to wow your audience, this is when we wish you good luck!

Frequently Asked Questions

Good question. Don’t hang back in messaging us for a free proposal and a price. We’ll cost it to the specifics of your project. Include things like number of slides, intricacy of design and the extent of your content.
